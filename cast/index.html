Dark Matter

One

One was originally Derrick Moss, CEO of CoreLactic Industries. When Three (pre-amnesia) murdered his wife, he got plastic surgery and assumed the identity of criminal named Jace Corso to gain passage on the Raza to kill Three.

01

Two

Two is an advanced biosynthetic organism, manufactured by Dwarf Star Technologies. She is a max of human and artificial parts with corrective nanites that give her advanced healing abilities. She's something of an illegal prototype - if the authorites ever discovered her existence, it's likely she would be hunted down and destroyed.

02

Three

At one point, he was hired for rebel missions for the Ferrous Corporation, during which he fell in love with a woman named Sarah.
He murdered One's wife, but the circumstances are unknown.

03

Four

He is the crown prince Ishida Ryo, who is on the run for supposedly killing his father the Emperor. His vengeful stepmother actually set him up.

Five

At some point before the crew's memories were wiped, she absorbed all their memories. This resulted in having flashes of memory from the pre-amnesia days, but she doesn't know whose they are (including if they are her own).
Pre-amnesia, she was a homeless teenager who snuck onboard the Raza to get medical supplies. She was caught by a pre-amnesia Three and locked up.

Six

Griffin Jones was a key operative of a resistance group tricked by the group's leader, The General, into destroying a space station with thousands aboard.
